A renovation photoshoot,

I was tasked by a Port Moody, BC-based renovation company Revitalize Home Repair & Renovations to take photos of my newly renovated home. This was my first photography commission.

I learnt the basics of lighting, how to stage a room, and the importance of composition to focus on certain elements in a space.
