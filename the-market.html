A board game project,

The Market is a board game prototype created for the final project in IAT 210. It aims to be a casual farming simulator where players aims to be the first to make their chosen country's local foods.

It's a turn-based tile board game where each tile has its own action when you land on them. You can gather resources, plant or bake foods, and sabotage other players' progress.

Our lead designers did an excellent job creating the board and all of the assets while others worked on the lore and game mechanics. Everyone helped in the creation of the overall game. My specific high-level contributions to this project are as follows:
- All playtesting and quantitative analysis
- Research on local food products for each country
- Creation of explicit rules
- Banner and Guidebook designs
