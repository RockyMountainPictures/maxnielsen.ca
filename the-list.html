A 5-minute short film,

The List applies everything we learned in film class IAT 202. From creating a story to the VFX, our group of four created this short film from scratch.

It was a tough journey to finish this final project. Two group members were not motivated to help. I attempted to involve them in the project with limited success. I had to create the story, plot and shots list by myself. I managed the location, props, and coordinated reshoots. I directed, shot, edited, and colour corrected.

We were weeks behind schedule and only had a day to edit everything together. We submitted what I felt looked like a rough draft.

I learnt a lot and tried my best to lead our group, but there are many leadership skills I must still learn to manage a unmotivated group.

UPDATE: After classes ended, a group member and I decided to re-edit the film into a directors cut. This is still in development and will come out soon(ish).
