Tough Crowd

2021
IAT 100
Individual Project

^ Details

An animation project,

Based on the song Tough Crowd by Lin Manuel Miranda from the motion picture Vivo, this animated lyrics video was my final project for animation class IAT 100.

I used Affinity Designer for asset creation and Adobe After Effects for animation.

Tough Crowd was chosen for its fast pace and complex lyrics to challenge my animation skills. It allowed me to be unrestricted by the song's tempo while providing some vibrant ideas to explore. 

I applied the principles of animation, basic compositional techniques, and principles of motion design in Tough Crowd.
